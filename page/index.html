Tower Defense? 2D Action? Action Tower Defense!

  • You have enjoyed watching waves of enemies get crushed by your towers?
  • But you don't have an hour to waste building mazes and buying upgrades?
  • Then this is the game for you!
  • Circle Gladiator Army Arena (CGAA) combines 2D action with Tower Defense to achieve a unique blend of speed and strategy.

Play the story of a young, rebellious circle that faces the challenging task of defending his new found blue friends against multiple camps of blue-hating aggressors. Can you convince them to stop or do they need to feel the power of your towers?
The king watches you fight for your life from his guarded chamber and laughs. But once all the camps are conquered, he will know that he messed with the wrong circle!

Fight the waves and conquer the chaos!

  • Get ready to face the ambush of four different camps at the same time.
  • Closely monitor the paths of the arriving waves and place your towers for maximum effect .

But beware. Just survival is not enough!

Bring the fight to them!

Once your friends seem save, the next challenge awaits you. These blue-haters might think that they are secure in their camps, with their buildings and their guards. But you know better.
Strategically sell your towers and place new towers around and inside their camps to show them, that destruction is at their doorstep.

Earn respect and leadership!

  • They might be haters, but if you accept the quest to kill their rivals, they will respect you.
  • If you show them that you should not be messed with, they will no longer attack you and you are able to cooperate and choose targets for their next waves.

However, your duty is not done!

Get your revenge!

You nearly are the king of the arena. Just one problem: The current king. He laughed at you, when you where trying to survive. But now the doors to his chamber are opened.

Lead your army of circles, show your skills with the towers and build a grave for this arrogant monarch.

CGAA: The arena awaits you!

Features:

  • Action: Move quickly to deal damage but not take it. Choose targets carefully to increase your soul-counter with killing small units until you are able to take on bigger ones.
  • Tactics: Place your limited amount of towers wisely to protect your friends and not die but also be able to progressively conquer the enemy camps.
  • Strategy: Choose which camps to cooperate with and which to destroy. The wrong choice might lead to your camp being overrun.

Content:

  • Computer-generated minimal graphics
  • Two types of towers
  • Three types of enemies
  • Endless waves of enemies
  • Randomized camp, building and guardians setup
  • Build and Attack Modes
  • Cooperation System with Destruction-Quests
  • Controlling the wave-targets of cooperating camps
  • Full Gameflow: survival-conquest-king
  • Many minutes of chaotic fun
